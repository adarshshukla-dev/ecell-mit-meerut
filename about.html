What is E-Cell?

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Meerut Institute of Technology is dedicated to fostering the entrepreneurial mindset among students. We believe entrepreneurship can be cultivated through guidance, resources, and opportunities. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Our mission is to build an ecosystem that encourages innovation and supports students in transforming their ideas into viable ventures with the help of mentors, industry experts, and networking. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
