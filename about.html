What is E-Cell?

Understanding the entrepreneurship cell and its mission at MIT

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Meerut Institute of Technology is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. We believe that entrepreneurship is a mindset that can be cultivated through proper guidance, resources, and opportunities.

Our mission is to create an ecosystem that encourages innovative thinking and helps students transform their ideas into viable business ventures. We connect students with industry experts, mentors, and investors who guide them throughout their entrepreneurial journey.

Through workshops, events, and programs, we equip students with essential entrepreneurial skills and provide access to co-working spaces, seed funding, and mentorship to help startups grow and scale.
