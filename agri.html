Agri Start UP Today

Empowering agri‑entrepreneurs through mentorship, infrastructure, and resources to build sustainable agricultural ventures and agri‑tech solutions.

What is Agri Start UP Today?

An initiative dedicated to supporting agricultural innovation, connecting farmers, agripreneurs, and students with guidance, training, and incubation support to scale sustainable agri‑businesses.

Supported by Meerut Udyami Foundation and hosted at the Agri‑Business Incubator at Meerut Institute of Technology, this program bridges the gap between farm‑level ideas and scalable business outcomes. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

What You Get

Mentorship & Guidance

Expert support from industry professionals and agripreneurs.

Resources & Tools

Access to market research, labs, prototyping tools, and co‑working space.

Networking & Exposure

Connect with incubators, investors, and sustainability partners.

Workshops & Training

Grow your agri‑business skills with hands‑on workshops and bootcamps.

Ready to Grow?

If you’re an aspiring agripreneur, farmer innovator, or student with a vision for agri‑impact, join Agri Start UP Today and be part of the future of sustainable agriculture.
